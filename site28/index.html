The History of This Website

At first, there was nothing. Just an empty void of digital silence.

Then, the <!DOCTYPE html> declaration appeared — a proclamation that this page shall follow the rules of HTML5.

Shortly after, the <html> tag emerged, marking the birth of the document's structure. It proudly declared its language: English.

Inside, the <head> was formed — the brain of the page. It gained a <meta charset="UTF-8"> to speak many characters, and a <title> to name itself.

A <style> tag joined in, bringing color, typography, and layout — giving life to form.

Then came the <body>, the heart of the page. It received a <h1> — its proud headline. Paragraphs followed, one by one, telling this very story.

And thus, a simple page was born. Not much — just some structured light and digital ink — but with a soul of its own.
